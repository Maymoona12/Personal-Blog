First Challenge

By Maymoona Bani Oudeh

One of the significant challenges individuals face after graduation is transitioning from academic life to the professional world.

This period is often marked by the difficulty of securing a job in a competitive market, where the skills learned in college may not fully match industry demands.

Graduates also face financial pressures, such as repaying student loans and managing living expenses, which add to the stress. Additionally, adapting to a full-time job while striving to maintain a work-life balance can be overwhelming.

Building a professional network and finding a sense of purpose in this new phase are crucial steps to overcoming these hurdles and achieving career growth.
